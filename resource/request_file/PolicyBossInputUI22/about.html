About Us

Serving the diverse insurance needs of customers pan india since 2002

Landmark caters to Millions of Customers and is partmered with all major insurance service providers, having presence in 800+ Locations

We are one of the largest retail insurance brokers in India with over 35% CAGR over last 6 years. Our nationwide chain of offices, staffed with experienced team, makes buying insurance and registering a claim a convenient pleasure. We are also India's leading motor insurance broker in the retail segment. PolicyBoss.com is the online presence of Landmark Insurance Brokers.

At Landmark Insurance Brokers, we pride ourselves on a seamless claims service to our clients which extends from the lodging of claim to its subsequent realisation, supported by a dedicated team of claims experts. The strong relationships with insurance service providers across product types ensure that we can offer the state of art solution to our customers, which is both relevant and right sized, partnering them in their growth.

Driven by the vision to emerge as a leading insurance intermediary for retail and corporate consumers across regions & segments, we at Landmark Insurance Brokers are committed to nurture and grow clients through a great buying experience, delivered through technological interventions.

Our Mission

To be India's No 1 Insurance broker offering solutions that improve the lives of customers, to form lasting relationships with partners and to build a trusted brand that will play a pivotal role in the expansion of the insurance industry.

Our Vision

To be India's broker of choice for innovative insurance products with a reputation for quality of service and speed of implementation and execution.
